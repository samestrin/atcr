# How should tasks targeting external repos be handled when th

## Decision

Descope them explicitly via /clarifications before sprint start, not abandoned mid-sprint. Record the descope decision in the epic clarifications section with the reason (repo does not exist in workspace, cannot be created/CI-gated/merged from this branch). Mark the descoped tasks (e.g. T3, T4, community half of T7) as OUT of scope in the in-scope/out-of-scope boundary section. This prevents the sprint from being blocked and ensures the alignment check correctly classifies the omission as pre-documented rather than drift. The community-external work becomes a separate work item.
