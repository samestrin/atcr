# Why do withinSeverityCeiling and meetsSeverityFloor fail in 

## Decision

meetsSeverityFloor fails CLOSED on rank-0 (unknown) severity; withinSeverityCeiling (sprint 32.1) fails OPEN on the same case. Safe only because generateFixes always runs the floor check before the ceiling check in the skip chain, so an unranked finding is already rejected before the ceiling sees it. Flagged by two adversarial reviews as a latent trap if the ordering is ever reversed or the predicate reused standalone. Documented as a comment co-located at the call site in internal/verify/executor.go rather than enforced in code.
