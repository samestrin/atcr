# Why must assistant-message tool-call arguments be re-seriali

## Decision

FunctionCall.Arguments is json.RawMessage and preserves inbound wire form. A litellm provider emitting object-form args causes the echo to re-send as object; strict validators reject it on the next turn. Canonicalize to OpenAI string form before appending resp.Message. Also guard finish_reason length/content_filter with empty assistant turn as failure, not StatusOK.
