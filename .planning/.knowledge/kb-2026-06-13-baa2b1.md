# How should path-jail and snapshot guards canonicalize roots 

## Decision

Use EvalSymlinks, not Clean, for jail roots and temp-dir guards. filepath.Clean does not follow symlinks; on macOS /tmp -> /private/tmp, so a HasPrefix guard using Clean(/tmp) fails to match a MkdirTemp base under /private/tmp. Canonicalize at construction with EvalSymlinks.
