# What does the C2 'one persona unit, one resolution chain' de

## Decision

When a feature needs both a binding (provider/model + catalog metadata) and custom content (a model-tuned prompt) for the same entity, the temptation is a second delivery mechanism alongside the existing one. This sprint's locked C2 decision converged instead: a persona is one self-contained installable unit — YAML binding + co-located .md prompt, installed atomically together — resolved through the existing single-precedence ResolvePersona chain, with built-ins as embedded copies of the same unit format rather than a divergent second format. Lesson: when extending an existing resolution/delivery mechanism, prefer converging two formats into one over building parallel machinery around the split — it keeps precedence rules, collision handling, and test surface singular, and forces fixing rather than working around underlying divergences (like the PersonasDir/resolver-dir mismatch).
