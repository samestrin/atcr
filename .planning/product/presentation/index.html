The Problem

Single-model review is flawed.

"Smaller local models often missed planted bugs that frontier models caught."

— Creator of lgtmaybe (a single-model reviewer)

  • If one model misses it, no one catches it.
  • You're trusting one model's judgment blindly.
  • Conflicts and disagreements are flattened away.
The Core Idea

Agreement is the confidence score.

A finding two independent models both caught is worth more than either model's opinion alone.

How it works

Three commands. One artifact.

01 · review

Fan out

Dispatch payload to a heterogeneous roster of personas in parallel.

02 · reconcile

Merge deterministically

Cluster, dedupe, score confidence, and preserve disagreements.

03 · report

Read verdict

Render high-confidence output to disk as markdown or JSON.
