## Warm-up (2 min)

- How did the two weeks go overall?
- Which features did you actually use?

## Force-rank (10 min)

Rank the three pilot features from most to least valuable, and say why:

1. Shared team registry (`ATCR_REGISTRY_URL`)
2. `atcr history`
3. `atcr audit-report`

> Capture the ranking **and the reasoning** — the reasoning is the evidence
> that goes in the memo.

## Core questions (13 min)

1. **Which feature would you miss most if it were removed?**
2. **How do you solve this today** (without atcr)?
3. **What would make you pay?**
   - At what monthly price is it a no-brainer?
   - At what monthly price is it too expensive?
4. **Minimum feature set for a paid launch** — what has to be in the box
   before you'd put it on a card?

## Wrap (5 min)

- Anything that almost made you stop using it?
- Anything missing that you expected to be there?
- Can I quote you (attributed / anonymous) in an internal write-up?
