- **Two weeks of normal use** on one of your repos.

- **One 30-minute exit interview** at the end of week 2.

Terms:

- **No obligation.** You can stop at any point.
- **Free during the pilot only.** No card, no commitment.

onboarding call and I'll send an invite.
